Software Design

For our project, we created an android app that gives you real-time directions using the google maps API.

Design

First, our app asks the user where they want to go. It assumes the user is walking to their destination. Then, it gets directions from google maps and parses the information into specific steps. These steps consist of a turn direction (right or left) and a location. The app continually checks whether the user has reached the location of the nect step then sends a signal over bluetooth to the ardiuno in their shoe that controls the motors.

Opening Screen

The first screen you see when you open the app is a map of where you are.

Path.

Once you choose a destination, the app draws a path from where you are to where you want to go to visually represent the directions

Turn by Turn Navigation

Once you start the directions, you can put your phone away, because your phone will continually check its location against each step of the directions to ensure that it tells you the right thing through the shoe as you go.
